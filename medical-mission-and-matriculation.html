SUMAS to Host Free Medical Mission and 4th Matriculation Ceremony

Published by Naija University Chronicle | January 2026

The State University of Medical and Applied Sciences (SUMAS), Igbo-Eno, Enugu State, in collaboration with the Rotary Foundation of Rotary International and the Enugu State Government, has announced two major events scheduled to take place in January 2026.

Free Medical Mission and Surgical Services

The Free Medical Mission will hold from Thursday, 16th January to Friday, 30th January 2026 at the SUMAS Teaching Hospital (SUMATH), Igbo-Eno, located along Orba–Obollo Afor Express Road, Udenu Local Government Area of Enugu State.

The medical outreach will provide free services in the following areas:

  • General Surgery (Breast, thyroid, gallbladder, appendix, hernia, GIT and laparoscopic surgeries)
  • Ophthalmology and Eye Surgery (Cataracts, lens replacement, pterygium)
  • Obstetrics and Gynaecology
  • Dental Care and Surgery
  • Ear, Nose and Throat (ENT) Surgery
  • Orthopaedic Surgery (Corrective and implant surgeries)

Registration for interested beneficiaries begins on Monday, 12th January 2026 at the same venue. Enquiries can be made through the University Public Relations Office via 08063793582 or 08038470813.

4th Matriculation Ceremony

The management of the State University of Medical and Applied Sciences (SUMAS) has also announced its 4th Matriculation Ceremony for newly admitted students of the 2025/2026 academic session.

The ceremony is scheduled to take place on Saturday, 17th January 2026, by 10:00am at the SUMAS campus, Igbo-Eno.

The Vice-Chancellor, Professor James Chukwuma Ogbonna, FAS, on behalf of the university management, staff, and students, has cordially invited members of the university community and the general public to witness the formal induction of new students.

These events underscore the commitment of the Enugu State Government, under the leadership of His Excellency, Barrister Peter Mbah, to quality education, accessible healthcare, and community development.
